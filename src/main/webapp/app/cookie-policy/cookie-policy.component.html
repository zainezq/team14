6. Cookies used on this website

The following cookies/similar technologies are used on this website:

Cookies owned by the operator of this website:

Session Cookies:

- Purpose: These cookies are essential for the operation of our website. They are used to maintain user session data, such as remembering items added to a shopping cart or user login status during a browsing session.

- Expiry: These cookies are temporary and expire once the user closes the browser.

 

Persistent Cookies:

- Purpose: We use persistent cookies to remember user preferences and provide personalized features. These cookies may store information such as language preferences, font size preferences, or customization settings.

- Expiry: These cookies remain on the user's device for a specified period, even after the browser is closed.

Authentication Cookies:

- Purpose: Authentication cookies are used to authenticate users and ensure that only authorized users can access certain parts of our website or perform specific actions.

- Expiry: These cookies may have varying expiration periods, depending on the specific authentication requirements.

 

Security Cookies:

- Purpose: Security cookies help detect and prevent security risks, such as unauthorized access or fraudulent activities, by monitoring user activity and identifying suspicious behavior .

- Expiry: These cookies may have varying expiration periods, depending on the specific security requirements.
